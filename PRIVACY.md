# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 19, 2026

Me Gusta ("we", "our", or "the app") is built and operated by Wyatt Flewelling. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use Me Gusta, how we use it, and the rights you have over your data.

We believe privacy policies should be readable. If anything here is unclear, email us at **wyattflew@yahoo.com** and we'll explain.

---

## 1. What we collect

### Information you provide
- **Username** — a public name you choose. Required to use the app.
- **Photos of meals** — the images you choose to post.
- **Captions** — text you add to your posts.
- **Social handles** (optional) — your Snapchat and Instagram usernames, if you choose to share them on your profile.
- **Reports** — if you report a user or post, we save your report along with your account ID for moderation purposes.

### Information generated by your use of the app
- **An anonymous account ID** assigned by Firebase Authentication. We do not collect your email, phone number, or real name.
- **Friend relationships** — who you have added as a friend and who has added you.
- **Likes** — which posts you have liked.
- **Friend requests** — pending requests you've sent or received.
- **Blocked users** — a private list of accounts you've blocked.
- **Timestamps** — when posts were created.

### Information stored only on your device
- **Meal history (local copy)** — for fast access to your own posts.
- **Streak data** — calculated from your post timestamps.
- **Notification preferences** — whether you've enabled meal reminders.
- **Image cache** — temporary copies of photos you've already viewed.

### What we do NOT collect
- We do not collect your email address.
- We do not collect your real name.
- We do not collect your location.
- We do not access your contacts.
- We do not access any photos beyond the ones you explicitly choose to post.
- We do not track you across other apps or websites.
- We do not use advertising identifiers (IDFA).

---

## 2. How we use your information

We use your information only to:
- Make the app work — show your posts, your friends' posts, and your profile.
- Authenticate your device so only you can post under your account.
- Enforce community safety (review reports, honor blocks).
- Improve the app by fixing bugs we identify in anonymous error logs.

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers, marketers, or any other third party for their own use.

---

## 3. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored on **Google Firebase** servers (Firestore, Firebase Storage, Firebase Authentication), which is a service provided by Google LLC. Firebase is bound by its own privacy commitments and security standards. You can read Google's privacy policy at [https://policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

Server locations may include the United States and other regions where Google operates Firebase.

---

## 4. Sharing with third parties

We share data only with services strictly required to run the app:

- **Firebase (Google)** — hosts your account, posts, friends, and images.
- **Apple StoreKit** — processes Me Gusta Pro in-app purchases. Apple's privacy policy applies to purchase data: [https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

We do not share your data with any other party.

---

## 5. Public vs. private information

When you use Me Gusta:
- Your **username, posts, captions, meal types, and like counts** are visible to other signed-in users of the app. Treat anything you post as public to the Me Gusta community.
- Your **friend list, blocked users, and friend requests** are private. Other users cannot see them.
- Your **social handles**, if you add them, are visible to other users who view your profile.

---

## 6. Your rights and controls

You can, at any time, from inside the app:

- **Edit your username** — Profile → ⋯ menu → Edit Name.
- **Edit or remove social handles** — Profile → ⋯ menu → Social Links.
- **Delete a post** — long-press the post → Delete.
- **Block another user** — tap ⋯ on any post → Block.
- **Report content** — tap ⋯ on any post → Report.
- **Disable meal reminders** — Profile → ⋯ menu → toggle Meal Reminders off.
- **Delete your account permanently** — Profile → ⋯ menu → Settings & Privacy → Delete Account. This removes your posts, photos, friend connections, blocked list, and account itself within seconds. It cannot be undone.

If you live in the EU (GDPR), UK, California (CCPA), or another jurisdiction with a comprehensive privacy law, you also have the right to request a copy of your data, correct inaccurate data, or restrict our processing of your data. Email **wyattflew@yahoo.com** with your username and we will respond within 30 days.

---

## 7. Data retention

- **Your posts, profile, and friend connections** are kept until you delete them or delete your account.
- **Reports** are kept for up to 12 months for moderation purposes.
- **Anonymous error logs** are kept for up to 30 days.

When you delete your account, all of the above is removed immediately, except where we are legally required to retain it (which is extremely rare and would only apply to law enforcement requests).

---

## 8. Children's privacy

Me Gusta is not designed for or directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under those ages. If you believe a child has created an account, please email **wyattflew@yahoo.com** and we will remove it immediately.

---

## 9. Security

We use industry-standard security measures:
- All network traffic between the app and our servers is encrypted with TLS.
- Photos and data are stored in Firebase with access controlled by security rules — users can only read what they're authorized to and can only modify their own data.
- We do not store passwords because we use anonymous authentication.

No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable precautions. If we ever experience a data breach affecting your information, we will notify you within the timeframe required by law.
